# Cold Chain Stability Guide

Synthetic fixture only. This guide is not a clinical or commercial release authorization.

## Shipment CT-204 disposition rule

Shipment CT-204 contains a refrigerated study product with an approved transport range of 2 C to 8 C. If a single temperature excursion remains above 8 C for no more than 45 cumulative minutes and the peak stays below 12 C, quarantine the shipment while telemetry is reconciled. A pharmacist may release it only after confirming sensor identity, calibration status, duration, and peak temperature.

Reject CT-204 if the excursion exceeds 45 cumulative minutes, reaches 12 C or higher, has an unresolved sensor-identity mismatch, or lacks a valid calibration record. Record the evidence and reviewer identity with the final disposition.

## Evidence handling

Use the telemetry record for duration and peak values. Do not infer missing readings. Cite the stability-rule chunk and the telemetry chunk when explaining a disposition.
