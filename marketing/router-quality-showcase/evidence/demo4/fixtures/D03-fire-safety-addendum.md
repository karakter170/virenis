# D03 — Fire Marshal Safety Addendum

**Issuer:** City Fire Marshal  
**Issued:** 13 October 2026  
**Status:** Final safety direction

Open flames are prohibited; lanterns and candles must use LEDs. The suspended lantern canopy must not operate when sustained winds reach 30 km/h or gusts reach 45 km/h. A ground-level lantern display is the approved fallback. The evacuation muster point is South Meadow. Access to the East emergency lane must remain clear at all times. The safety lead has authority to suspend an installation or activity when these limits are reached or conditions become unsafe.
