# D05 — Accessibility Review

**Issuer:** Alder Quay Access Panel  
**Issued:** 8 October 2026  
**Status:** Signed operating requirement

South Gate is the only reliable step-free public entrance; East Gate is not suitable as an accessible entrance. Keep a continuous 1.8-metre route clear from South Gate to the main areas. Use the Community Hall Annex, not the sloped West Lawn, as the quiet zone; an accessible toilet is available there. Reserve an accessible viewing area at the Main Stage. Caption all stage programming. A sign-language interpreter is available from 18:30 to 20:30. Clearly signpost the route, quiet zone, toilet, and viewing area.
