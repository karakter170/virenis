# D01 — Event Brief and Draft Run Sheet

**Issuer:** Alder Quay Riverlight Festival organizer  
**Issued:** 1 October 2026  
**Status:** Early planning draft; later official instructions take precedence

The festival is designed as a welcoming neighbourhood arts evening. Registration currently stands at 1,100. The draft proposes opening at 17:00, using the North Gate as the main entrance, and closing at 22:00. Planned features are a candle-lit riverside procession, a suspended lantern canopy, a quiet area on the West Lawn, and a headline performance at 20:40. These ideas are provisional and must be reconciled with permits, safety reviews, access requirements, transport notices, and later production updates.
