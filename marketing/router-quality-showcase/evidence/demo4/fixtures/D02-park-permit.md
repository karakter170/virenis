# D02 — Final Park Permit

**Issuer:** City Parks Department  
**Issued:** 12 October 2026  
**Status:** Final and controlling for park operations

No more than 900 people may be onsite at once. Every open public entrance must use clicker counts, with the combined count reported to operations. Amplified sound must end by 20:45 and the site must be clear by 21:30. The East service lane is an emergency route and must remain unobstructed. At least two certified first aiders must be onsite whenever the public is admitted. These conditions replace conflicting times or capacity assumptions in planning drafts.
