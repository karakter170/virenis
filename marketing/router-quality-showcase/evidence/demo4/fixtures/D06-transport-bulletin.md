# D06 — Roadworks and Transport Bulletin

**Issuer:** City Transport Control  
**Issued:** 16 October 2026  
**Status:** Final event update

The North approach will be closed from 16:00 to 23:00, so North Gate cannot serve arriving or departing visitors. The event shuttle will stop at the East curb. The accessible drop-off point is at South Gate. The last scheduled bus from the South stop leaves at 21:20. Cycle parking is beside East Gate. Rideshare vehicles cannot enter the quay and should use the signed remote pickup point. Public communications must not direct visitors to the North approach.
