# D09 — Vendor Health and Allergen Inspection

**Issuer:** City Environmental Health Officer  
**Issued:** 15 October 2026  
**Status:** Conditional approval

Place the vendor row on the East hardstand. Cooking is not allowed beneath fabric structures. Moon Bowl may serve only if it submits an acceptable temperature log by noon on Saturday; approval is not yet confirmed. Hearth & Grain uses shared preparation space and must not describe its food as nut-free. Vendors must display cross-contact information. The public water-refill point is at the Community Hall Annex. Operations must remove any vendor that fails the stated conditions.
