# D08 — River Habitat and Noise Agreement

**Issuer:** River Habitat Authority  
**Issued:** 5 October 2026  
**Status:** Binding event condition

Maintain a 25-metre dark buffer around the reed bed. No lighting may point across the water after 20:30. Amplified sound is allowed only at the Main Stage. Any lantern procession must follow the inland path, not the river edge. Before gates open, the wildlife steward must inspect and approve the marked buffer boundary. These restrictions apply to artists, vendors, staff, and visitors and cannot be relaxed by the organizer's draft programme.
